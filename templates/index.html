NUSOM.data is a website for accessing and writing records of diseases in different countries. Records are created by public servants who are studying and/or working at Nazarbayev University School of Medicine. Records are created on diseases which NUSOM doctors are specialized at. You can find more information via links in the naviagtion bar.
